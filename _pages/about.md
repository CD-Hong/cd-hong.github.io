I am an M.A. student in the Department of Economics at Seoul National University.

My primary research interests include financial markets, financial intermediation, and their interaction with the macroeconomy. Recently, I have been particularly focused on mutual funds and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). I am currently advised by Professor Jaewon Choi and work as his research assistant. 

Prior to graduate school, I worked as a full-time junior economist and portfolio manager at the [Bank of Korea](https://www.bok.or.kr/eng/), the central bank of South Korea. I managed investments in U.S. Agency MBS (primarily), CMOs, Treasury bonds, and futures, overseeing a portfolio valued at USD 30 billion. I am also an avid soccer fan and have been a loyal Liverpool FC supporter since the seasons when they finished 6th, 8th, and 6th in the Premier League!
