My research interests include international finance, financial intermediation, and their interaction with the macroeconomy. I am a second-year M.A. student in Economics at Seoul National University, advised by and working as a research assistant for Professor Jaewon Choi.
Before graduate school, I was a junior economist and portfolio manager at the Bank of Korea. I managed a USD 30 billion portfolio of South Korea's foreign reserves, primarily comprising U.S. Agency MBS, Treasury bonds, and futures.
For more information, please see my CV. My Korean first name is 창덕 (Chang-Deok), but I go by CD. Please feel free to contact me.
