Using a dynamic shift–share framework, this study traces Incheon’s pivot toward a service oriented economy, where regional competitiveness lifted services’ shares in both value added and employment, then documents the pandemic era reversal, with transportation and warehousing dragging service competitiveness while manufacturing and construction proved resilient. Building on these diagnostics, we outline a pragmatic upgrade path: grow high productivity, knowledge based services and accelerate digital transformation across tradables to raise productivity, restore regional advantage, and anchor sustainable growth.
